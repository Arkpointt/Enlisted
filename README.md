# Enlisted – Military Service Mod

Serve as a soldier in any lord's warband. Follow orders, earn wages, climb ranks, and retire with honor.

## Requirements
- Mount & Blade II: Bannerlord v1.3.6+
- Bannerlord.Harmony mod

## Installation
1. Download from [Releases](../../releases)
2. Extract to `<Bannerlord>\Modules\Enlisted\`
3. Enable Bannerlord.Harmony and Enlisted in the launcher

## How to Play

### Enlist
1. Find any lord on the campaign map
2. Talk to them → "I wish to serve in your warband"
3. Your party hides and you follow the lord automatically

### Serve
- **Wages**: Paid daily based on rank
- **Battles**: Join automatically when your lord fights (+25 XP per battle, +1 XP per kill)
- **Promotions**: Tier 2 unlocks formation choice (Infantry/Archer/Cavalry/Horse Archer)
- **Duties**: Pick daily assignments for bonus XP and wages
- **Equipment**: Culture-specific gear unlocks at each tier via the Quartermaster

### Leave & Return
- Request leave through your lord (14 days max)
- Return before time expires or face desertion penalties
- If your lord dies or is captured, you have 14 days to find a new commander in the same faction

### Retire
After 252 days (3 Bannerlord years):
- **Retire**: 10,000 gold + relations with your lord and faction
- **Re-enlist**: 20,000 gold bonus, extend service 1 year

## What Changes While Enlisted
- You join the lord's kingdom as a mercenary (no native mercenary pay - only mod wages)
- No personal loot (spoils go to your lord)
- No starvation (lord provides food)
- No clan expenses shown (only your wages)
- No Order of Battle screen
- Naval support: Follow your lord onto ships (Naval War Expansion compatible)

## Troubleshooting
Logs are in `<Bannerlord>\Modules\Enlisted\Debugging\`:
- `enlisted.log` - Main activity log
- `conflicts.log` - Mod compatibility info
