[size=4][b][color=#b85e34]WHAT IS THIS MOD?[/color][/b][/size]
Enlisted transforms Bannerlord into a deep soldier career simulator. Instead of managing an empire, you serve one. Enlist with any Lord, follow their orders, earn your wages, and let the world unfold around you.

It features [b]275+ narrative content pieces[/b] spanning [b]9 military ranks[/b] from fresh recruit to senior commander, built to be lightweight, open-source, and compatible with naval mods and UI overhauls.

[b][color=#c9a96e]A Note on Open Source:[/color][/b]
Contributions are welcome. Whether it's bug fixes, features, or balance tweaks, PRs are encouraged on [url=https://github.com/Arkpointt/Enlisted]GitHub[/url].

[size=4][b][color=#b85e34]MAIN FEATURES[/color][/b][/size]
[b][color=#c9a96e]‚öîÔ∏è THE LIFE OF A SOLDIER[/color][/b]
[b]Simple Enlistment:[/b] Ask any lord, "I wish to serve in your warband," and you're in.
[b]True Immersion:[/b] You march, eat, and get paid by your lord. Live inside their campaign loop.
[b]Crash Guards:[/b] Built-in safety checks for sieges, encounters, captivity, and naval integration.
[b]Companions:[/b] Your companions stay with you during service. Toggle who fights vs who stays back.
[b]Temporary Leave:[/b] Visit family, conduct trade, rest and recover. Just don't go AWOL.

[b][color=#c9a96e]üõ°Ô∏è CHAIN OF COMMAND[/color][/b]
[b]Automatic Deployment (T1-T6):[/b] Auto-assigned to formation based on your equipped weapons. The Order of Battle screen is suppressed to keep you in the moment.
[b]16 Military Orders:[/b] Chain of command issues orders from sergeants, lieutenants, and your lord.
[list][*][b]Basic Duties (T1-T3):[/b] Auto-assigned mandatory tasks (guard duty, camp patrol, firewood detail, equipment checks, muster, sentry).
[*][b]Advancement Orders (T4+):[/b] Optional missions you can accept or decline (lead patrol, scout route, train recruits, escort duty).[/list]
[b]85 Order Events:[/b] Dynamic events fire during order execution with 15-35% chance per duty period.
[b]Follow Orders:[/b] F1-F9 keys are disabled. You don't give the orders; you follow them.

[b][color=#c9a96e]üìà RANK & PROGRESSION (9 TIERS)[/color][/b]
Climb the ladder through [b]9 distinct tiers[/b] based on battle XP, service time, battles fought, reputation, and discipline:
[b]T1 ‚Üí T2 ‚Üí T3 ‚Üí T4 ‚Üí T5 ‚Üí T6 ‚Üí T7 ‚Üí T8 ‚Üí T9[/b]
Culture-specific rank titles, narrative "proving events" at key promotions, equipment tier unlocks, and officer privileges at higher ranks.

[b][color=#c9a96e]üí∞ PAY & MUSTER SYSTEM[/color][/b]
[b]12-Day Muster Cycle:[/b] Wages accrue daily in a ledger, paid out every 12 days at ceremonial muster events.
[b]8-Stage Muster Ceremonies:[/b] Immersive pay day events with stages including pay line, baggage check, inspection, recruit, promotion recap, and retinue management.
[b]Period Summaries:[/b] 12-day recaps with combat/training/orders/XP breakdown.
[b]Pay Tension System:[/b] When pay is late, tension rises and unlocks special choices.

[b][color=#c9a96e]‚õ∫ CAMP LIFE & DUTIES[/color][/b]
[b]Two-Layer Camp System:[/b]
[list][*][b]Background Simulation:[/b] Autonomous company life runs automatically (soldiers get sick, injured, or desert; equipment breaks; morale shifts).
[*][b]Camp Opportunities:[/b] 29 contextual player activities with fitness scoring that adapts to your preferences.[/list]
[b]38 Player-Initiated Decisions:[/b] Training (weapon drills, sparring), social (join the men, write letters), economic (gambling, loans), career (request audience, volunteer), information gathering (rumors, scouts), and more.
[b]Fatigue System:[/b] Actions accumulate fatigue; rest recovers it. Fatigue affects performance and event outcomes.
[b]Medical Care:[/b] 15 injury types with severity levels, treatment decisions, recovery chains, and surgeon interactions.

[b][color=#c9a96e]üõ†Ô∏è EQUIPMENT & QUARTERMASTER[/color][/b]
[b]Quartermaster System (10+ Subsystems):[/b]
[list][*]Equipment purchasing with reputation-based discounts (0-30%)
[*][b]6 Quality Tiers:[/b] Shoddy, Standard, Serviceable, Fine, Masterwork, Legendary
[*][b]Upgrade System:[/b] Sequential quality improvements with real stat bonuses using native ItemModifier
[*]Gauntlet Grid UI for browsing and upgrades
[*]Buyback service (30-65% value), tier gates by rank
[*][b]Officers Armory (T7+):[/b] Elite gear catalog for commanders[/list]
[b]Provisions & Rations:[/b] T1-T6 issued rations every 12 days. T7+ premium provisions shop with rank-based access.
[b]Baggage Train:[/b] Early enlistment inventory handling, baggage checks during muster, reclamation of personal gear upon discharge.

Officer (Commander 10+) ‚Ä¢ Scout (ScoutSkills 10+) ‚Ä¢ Medic (Surgery 10+) ‚Ä¢ Engineer (SiegeCraft 10+) ‚Ä¢ Operative (RogueSkills 10+) ‚Ä¢ NCO (SergeantCommandSkills 8+) ‚Ä¢ Soldier (default)

[list][*][b]Lord Reputation:[/b] Standing with your commanding lord

[b][color=#c9a96e]üèá COMMANDER'S RETINUE (T7+ OFFICERS)[/color][/b]
[b]Personal Military Force:[/b] Officers command a personal retinue at T7-T9.

[b]23 Narrative Events:[/b] 11 character development events, 6 post-battle incidents (heroism, casualties, desertion), 4 camp decisions (discipline, rewards, training).

[b][color=#c9a96e]üì∞ UI & REPORTING[/color][/b]

[size=4][b][color=#b85e34]INSTALLATION[/color][/b][/size]
[b][color=#c9a96e]Requirements:[/color][/b]
Mount & Blade II: Bannerlord (supported versions depend on the release you installed)
[url=https://www.nexusmods.com/mountandblade2bannerlord/mods/2006]Bannerlord.Harmony[/url] (required)

[b][color=#c9a96e]How to Install:[/color][/b]
1. Extract the [b]Enlisted[/b] folder to your [i]<Bannerlord>\Modules\[/i] directory.
2. Enable [b]Harmony[/b] and [b]Enlisted[/b] in the game launcher.
3. [b]Recommended Load Order:[/b]
   Harmony ‚Üí Native ‚Üí SandBoxCore ‚Üí SandBox ‚Üí StoryMode ‚Üí CustomBattle ‚Üí Enlisted

[b][color=#c9a96e]Optional Battle AI Submodule:[/color][/b]
If you want to disable the Battle AI, simply uncheck it in the Bannerlord launcher. There is no performance cost when disabled.

[b][color=#c9a96e]Configuration (Optional):[/color][/b]
You can tweak wages, XP thresholds, duties, and equipment pricing by editing the JSON files found in:
[i]Modules\Enlisted\ModuleData\Enlisted\Config\[/i]

[b][color=#c9a96e]Troubleshooting:[/color][/b]
Logs are generated in [i]<Bannerlord>\Modules\Enlisted\Debugging\[/i]

[b]Example path:[/b]
[i]C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Mount & Blade II Bannerlord\Modules\Enlisted\Debugging\[/i]

[b]Files to share when reporting issues:[/b]
[list][*][b]Session-A_*.log[/b] (latest session log)
[*][b]Conflicts-A_*.log[/b] (latest conflicts report)
[*][b]Current_Session_README.txt[/b] (points to current files)[/list]

[b]Error Codes:[/b] Enlisted uses stable error codes (e.g., [b][E-CAMPUI-031][/b]) that are searchable and consistent. Please include error codes when reporting issues!

[b]Common Issues:[/b]
[list][*][b]Mod menu options don't show:[/b] Confirm Bannerlord.Harmony is enabled and loaded before Enlisted.
[*][b]War Sails / naval features don't work:[/b] Check if War Sails (NavalDLC) expansion is enabled. Check session log for W-DLC-* warnings.
[*][b]Events don't fire:[/b] Confirm event systems are enabled in enlisted_config.json.[/list]
